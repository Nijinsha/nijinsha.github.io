Rules

  • Bubbly
    • Ring is provided at the floor
    • Participant should stand on the line provided.
    • From there hiss the bubble and make it pass through the ring.
    • No round restart will be provided.
  • Power Arm
    • Separate game for boys and girls.
    • Each participant should defeat 3 of his/her opponents.
    • Each winners will be awarded with Rs.50/-
    • No round restart will be provided.
  • Dont Not Light It!
    • Pass a disc attached to a stick through a wire.
    • Disk should not touch the wire.
    • LED will glow if the disc touches the wire.
    • Winners will be awarded by exciting gifts.
  • Touch The High
    • Each participant is provided with a sheet of paper
    • The participant should make a plane with the sheet provided.
    • Make the plane fly aiming the destination.
    • Winner will be the one who takes minimum time to complete the round.
  • Try Your Luck
    • Winner can be selected by matching first 6 ticket number.
    • Maximum number can select: 10.
    • Prize for matching the 6 numbers drawn
    • Participant wants to buy separate ticket for each draw.
  • Darts War!
    • Players take turns throwing at the numbers 1, 2, and so on, in sequence, until 5.
    • They try to score as many points as possible per turn (using all three darts. Only darts hitting the number in play count for score.
    • So, when the number 1 is in play, the highest score that can be achieved is three - if the player hits a 1 with each dart. However, when the number 5 is the number in play, the highest score could be a 21 - if the player hits 5 all three times.
    • Obviously, as the game progresses, the score gets higher. There are two ways to win: either have the highest score at the end of the game or score a shanghai and win automatically. A shanghai is hitting the single, double and triple of the number in play..
